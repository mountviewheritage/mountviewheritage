A Home With a Past

Set in the hills of Panchgani, Mount View Heritage is a home shaped by history and care. Originally built in the 1800s as a nunnery, it was converted into a hotel in 1923 and has remained largely unchanged since. With just 11 rooms, the same number from the beginning, the property has grown through preservation, not expansion. Our great-grandfather, Dhanjishaw Khambata, moved here from Mumbai with his family, choosing a quieter life in the hills. That sense of home continues today, from recipes passed down through generations to the warm, personal hospitality we share with every guest.
