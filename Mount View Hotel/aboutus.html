Letter from the family

At Mount View, we've always believed a stay should feel like coming home. Originally built as a nunnery in the 1800s and turned into a
hotel in 1923, Mount View has remained unchanged with its 11 rooms and timeless colonial charm. Our great-grandfather, Dhanjishaw
Khambata, moved here with his family, bringing with him traditions that continue even today. Many of our recipes have been passed
down through generations, cooked under our parents’ supervision - the same food we enjoy ourselves. Whether you're here for quiet
rest or celebration, we're delighted to welcome you into our family’s hillside haven.
