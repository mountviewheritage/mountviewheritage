Rediscovering Panchgani: The Art of Slow Travel

In a world that often celebrates speed and efficiency, the concept of slow travel invites us to hit the brakes. Panchgani, with its rolling hills, strawberry fields, and crisp mountain air, is perhaps one of the best places in Maharashtra to embrace this philosophy. Staying here isn't about ticking off observation points; it's about soaking in the environment.

Slow travel is about connection—connecting with local artisans, tasting the freshly picked produce, and taking long walks without a destination in mind. It turns a vacation into an immersion. Mount View Hotel, situated in the heart of this landscape, serves as the perfect base for such an exploration.

Instead of rushing from Table Land to Parsi Point, consider spending an afternoon reading by a colonial-style window or enjoying a cup of chai while watching the mist roll over the valley. The true charm of the hill station reveals itself in these quiet moments.

When you travel slowly, you leave a lighter footprint and take home richer memories. You begin to notice the changing colors of the sky, the distinct bird calls, and the architectural nuances of the old settlements. It transforms a simple getaway into a restorative retreat for the soul.
